Message Your Operator

Stay in touch with your MailPlus courier through the ShipMate portal. Send direct, one-way messages for updates like:

  • Change in Parcel Location: "Parcels have been left in a different place today."
  • Pick-up Schedule Updates: "Cancelled today's pick up. Parcels will be ready on the next scheduled day."
  • Early Parcel Readiness: "Parcels are ready earlier today if it works for you."
  • Special Pickup Requests: "We will close early today. Please pick up by 12PM if possible."
For more urgent requests or two-way communication, call your driver directly or call 1300 65 65 95.
